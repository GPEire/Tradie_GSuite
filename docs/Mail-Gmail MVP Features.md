# Key MVP Features \& Customer Positioning: AI Email Extension for Builders/Carpenters (Gmail/G Suite Focus)


***

## 1. Automated Project Email Grouping

**Feature Description:**
AI scans Gmail inbox, intelligently groups all customer emails by project/job, automatically tagging threads and aggregating relevant conversations (including attachments and client contacts).

- Detects related emails across multiple senders for the same project
- Flags new inquiries, ongoing requests, and historical client/job context
- Provides one-click access to project history and communications in Gmail sidebar

**Customer Positioning:**
*“Stay organized—never lose track of customer conversations, no matter how busy your inbox gets.”*
Ideal for builders juggling many concurrent projects; solves chaos of lost emails and missed follow-ups.

***

## 2. Quote Request Extraction \& Summarization

**Feature Description:**
AI parses incoming project/customer emails to extract scope, specifications, and requested variations, summarizing them into a clear quoting brief.

- Detects specs, plans, and change requests in unstructured text
- Summarizes client requirements for rapid in-house review
- Supports attachments (drawings, documents) via Google Drive integration

**Customer Positioning:**
*“Turn scattered emails into clear, actionable briefs—ready for quoting.”*
Addresses builder/carpenter frustration with time-consuming client spec extraction.

***

## 3. Automated Pricing Engine (Google Sheets Integration)

**Feature Description:**
Uses linked Google Sheets price lists to automate pricing for each project, including complex variants and options.

- AI matches extracted specs to relevant pricing line-items/assemblies
- Calculates customer-specific quotes; handles GST and AU pricing conventions
- Allows easy update of prices via shared Sheets interface

**Customer Positioning:**
*“Generate accurate project quotes automatically—no more manual spreadsheets.”*
Critical for small/medium teams needing speed, accuracy, and local compliance.

***

## 4. Internal Quote Review \& Approval Workflow

**Feature Description:**
Enables team leads/managers to review, edit, and approve draft quotes before sending to clients—all within Gmail.

- Interactive approval/rejection/comment workflow embedded in inbox
- Tracks approvals, rejections, and feedback per quote/project
- Supports team notifications and assignment logic (Google Chat/G Suite)

**Customer Positioning:**
*“Keep full control—quotes only reach clients after your review and approval.”*
Addresses critical risk of sending unreviewed or inaccurate pricing; adds compliance and peace of mind.

***

## 5. Automated Client Quote Delivery

**Feature Description:**
Upon approval, system auto-sends polished quote email to client, using branded templates with itemized details and custom disclaimers.

- Automated send reduces admin time and response delay
- Client receives clear, professional quote; supports follow-up tracking
- Record of all sent quotes remains for audit/tracking

**Customer Positioning:**
*“Respond faster and more professionally—win jobs with prompt, organized quoting.”*
Directly ties automation to business value: speed, reputation, and higher win rate.

***

## 6. Searchable Audit Trail \& Compliance Logging

**Feature Description:**
Stores all quote interactions (drafts, comments, approvals, sent emails) in a secure, searchable log for regulatory/audit purposes.

- Time/User stamps for every action
- Supports Australian compliance (GST, job codes, builder legal disclaimers)
- Simple export/reporting options for business or regulatory needs

**Customer Positioning:**
*“Stay compliant and covered—your communications and quotes are always logged, searchable, and report-ready.”*
Builds trust and reduces legal exposure for builder/carpenter businesses.

***

# Customer Positioning Summary

- **Critical Organizing Tool:** Never lose track of project communications; always know who said what, and when.
- **Speed \& Accuracy:** Move from client inquiry to delivered quote in minutes, not hours—without sacrificing precision.
- **Local Compliance, Local Support:** Designed for Australian builder conventions, GST pricing, and familiar workflows.
- **Full Control:** Empower team leads/managers with a robust approval process—avoid costly mistakes.
- **Easy Adoption:** Built to work inside the tools you already use (Gmail, Google Sheets), so staff actually use it.
- **Professional Edge:** Impress clients with faster, clearer responses—stand out from competitors stuck in manual admin.
